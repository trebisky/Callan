# The CWC handles the IV bus in its own unique way.
# Other systems I have looked at have 8x300 family chips
# that support a 2 step process.
# First you select the device by sending the device address using an XMIT to ivr or ivl
# Then you send (or receive) data from the device by a subsequent access to driv or sriv
# The Callan uses the extra ROM to select devices, which immediately respond to
# what is placed on the IV bus.
